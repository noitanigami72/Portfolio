## How to create a diagram
```mermaid
flowchart TD
	A[First stage of testing] --> B{Is it OK?};
	B -- Yes --> C[Deploy!];
	B -- No --> D[Back to tester];
	C ----> E[Enjoy your free time!];
	D ----> E[Enjoy your free time!];
```
